## Your Privacy Matters

**Last updated:** May 10, 2026

Milan Foundation ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our donors, volunteers, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website and services.

### Information We Collect

**Donor Information:**
- Full Name, Email, and Mobile Number.
- Permanent Account Number (PAN) for issuing 80G tax-exemption receipts.
- Mailing Address for physical receipts (if applicable).
- Donation history and frequency.

**General Inquiries:**
- Name, email, and messages sent through our contact forms.

**Information collected automatically:**
- Browser type, IP address, and device information.
- Website usage patterns and navigation history.

### How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- **Process Donations:** Securely handle your contributions and maintain accurate records.
- **Tax Compliance:** Issue 80G tax-exemption certificates as required by the Income Tax Department of India.
- **Impact Communication:** Send periodic newsletters and updates about our projects (you can opt out at any time).
- **Inquiry Response:** Address your questions about volunteering, programs, or support.
- **Accountability:** Maintain transparency in our operations and donor relations.

### Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data. Sensitive information like PAN is handled with high confidentiality and stored securely to prevent unauthorized access.

### Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only with:
- **Payment Gateways:** Trusted third-party processors to facilitate secure online donations.
- **Regulatory Authorities:** When required by law to comply with tax or non-profit regulations.
- **Internal Audit:** For maintaining transparency and financial accountability.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
- Withdraw your consent for receiving marketing communications.
- Request the deletion of your data (subject to legal record-keeping requirements).
